Emission Tracking Guidelines
What activities should I track?

Record activities that produce greenhouse gas emissions, including:

  • Energy consumption (electricity, gas, etc.)
  • Transportation (company vehicles, shipping, etc.)
  • Manufacturing processes
  • Business travel (flights, car rentals, etc.)
  • Waste management
  • Water usage
How to calculate emissions?

For accurate emissions calculation:

  • Use utility bills for energy consumption
  • Track fuel usage for transportation
  • Follow industry standards for manufacturing
  • Record mileage and transportation type for travel

For more detailed guidance, consult the Greenhouse Gas Protocol or industry-specific resources.
